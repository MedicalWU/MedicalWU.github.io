About Me

I am currently pursuing my Master degree in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC). My research focuses on biomedical robotics, fault diagnosis, and intelligent systems. I received my Bachelor degree in Mechanical Manufacturing and Automation from UESTC in 2023.

During my academic journey, I have developed expertise in signal processing, machine learning, and robotics. I am passionate about applying advanced technologies to solve real-world problems in healthcare and industrial automation.
